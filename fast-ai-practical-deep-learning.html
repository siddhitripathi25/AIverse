    fast.ai Practical Deep Learning

    Course Jeremy Howard Free

    Practical Deep Learning for Coders by fast.ai is a highly acclaimed, 100% free course designed to make deep learning accessible to anyone with basic coding experience. Instructed by Kaggle champion Jeremy Howard, the course follows a top-down teaching method where students build state-of-the-art models from the very first lesson.

    About this Course

    The course takes a code-first, top-down approach. Students learn to train high-quality computer vision, natural language processing, tabular data, and collaborative filtering models immediately, using PyTorch and the fastai library. The course then proceeds to peel back the layers to explain how SGD, loss functions, and activation layers function under the hood.

    Key Topics Covered

    • Image Classification and Segmentation (CNNs, ResNets)
    • Natural Language Processing: Text Classifiers & Language Models
    • Collaborative Filtering & Tabular Data Recommendation Systems
    • Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD) and Optimizer Foundations
    • Neural Network Architectures from scratch in PyTorch
    • Model Deployment to Production (Web interfaces, Hugging Face)

    Why this course is useful

    The top-down philosophy is incredibly effective. Rather than getting bogged down in linear algebra proofs before training models, you start by getting state-of-the-art results on datasets. This builds instant momentum and gives you practical, transferrable software engineering skills for AI development.

    Who should take it

    Software developers, hackers, and programmers who have at least one year of Python experience and want to apply deep learning to their projects. No advanced mathematical background or calculus is required.

    Start Learning with fast.ai

    Access all video lectures, course notebooks, documentation, and the highly active fast.ai community forum entirely for free on the official course site.
